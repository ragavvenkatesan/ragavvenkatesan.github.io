Through the lens

Let me be very clear. I am just a monkey with a DSLR camera. I have no idea about anything professional in photography. I bought a DSLR on a blackfriday 2015 deal. I was told I have an eye for photographs and I figured I would take some and share some. So don't expect any level of professionalism.

Some of these are albums, some just solo pictures. Click on them and they will take you to the respective flickr photostream with many such pictures. Also since I want to retain the size of these pictures I haven't scaled the webpage properly. I recommend this page to be viewed in computer screens lest you want a huge image in your mobile phone. Alternatively, you can view my flickr photostream directly.

Click on any picture and it will probably open up the entire flickr album.
